Snake Game - How to Play

Welcome to the Snake Game! Follow the instructions below to play:

  1. Use the arrow keys to control the snake's direction:
    • Left Arrow: Move left
    • Up Arrow: Move up
    • Right Arrow: Move right
    • Down Arrow: Move down
  2. Use the buttons in the bottom to control the snake's direction:
    • Button left: Move left
    • Button up: Move up
    • Button right: Move right
    • Button down: Move down
  3. Press the Space Bar to sprint and increase your speed temporarily.
  4. Click in the same direction as the snake to sprint on computer and touch devices.
  5. Avoid collisions with walls.
  6. Eat the food to earn points.
  7. Collect energy for earning more health.
  8. The game ends if the snake runs out of health.

Have fun playing the Snake Game!
